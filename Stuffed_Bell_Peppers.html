Stuffed Bell Pepper

Ingredients

For the filling:

  • 2lb Ground beef or Italian pork
  • 2cups cooked white rice
  • 2tbs ground garlic
  • 1tbs Onion powerder
  • 1/2tbs salt
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2tsp black pepper
  • 15oz Tomatoe sauce
  • 14.5oz or can diced tomatoes

Other Ingredients:

  • 6 bell peppers (any color of the rainbow)
  • 1cup of shreaded Mozzarella Cheese

Instructions

  1. Wash the Starch off the 2 Cups of white rice. Add washed rice and 2.5 cups of water to and instantpot, Cook for 5 mins on high preasure. Allow slow realease with keep warm off
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers. Take the core and seeds out. Throw away the steam and save the tops to dice and add to the stuffing.
  3. Bake them for 20mins at 350. (I baked them on a campchef woodwind at 350F smoke level 10 on the bottom rack, for a hint of smokeiness.)
  4. Brown the Ground beef. After meat has brown add the dice pepper topps.
  5. After 5mins add all the seasoning to the meat. Cook for 2mins
  6. Add the tomatoe sauce and diced tomatoes. Stir and simmer for 5mins.
  7. Grab a large bowl to mix the rice and other stuffing together
  8. Place the 6 baked peppers in 9x13 pan and fill the peppers with the stuffing. Bake for another 10mins.
  9. Add the mozzarella chees to the tops of the stuffe Peppers bake until the cheese melts about 5ish mins.

Any left over stuffing can top some baked potatoes.
